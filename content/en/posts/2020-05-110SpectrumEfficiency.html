What is Spectum Efficiency

I started my journey in the corporate world. Before coming to New Zealand, I was planning, drive testing, optimizing 3G, and 4G networks in Europe. As a Radio Frequency Engineer, one mostly works for the vendors. In my case, I have worked for:

  • Alcatel-Lucent(Nokia)
  • Huawei

My first job in New Zealand is where I met the regulator of this country for the first time. Radio Spectrum Management. I became an Approved Radio Engineer,ARE176. In the beginning, I optimized the licensing process that every site had to go through obtaining a spectrum license. It is here that I saw this gap that I wanted to address. When I joined Radio Spectrum Management, three years later, I quickly realized how deep the rabbit hole goes and that I have only scratched the surface.
